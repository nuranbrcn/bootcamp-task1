Sınav Academic system in Anatolian and Science High Schools A good high school is the door to a good university; shaped by the concept. Sınav Anatolian and Science High Schools, academic education takes place in two stages; With English education in the 9th and 10th grades, basic academics come to the fore. Culture, art, sports activities and club activities within the scope of Values Education and After School are also continuing. In the 11th and 12th grades, the preparatory studies of TYT, AYT and YKS, which are the university entrance exams, gain a serious momentum in this period. With TYT, AYT, YKS, which are applied regularly every week, an academic program is followed without the need for courses and private lessons, with course trials, question solution hours and study completion. Education continues intensively in the 11th and 12th grades, with the lessons taught without sacrificing English education and basic academic education. In Sınav High Schools, not one or some of the students go to university. Our school has the first place in Turkey in the University Examinations. Comprehensive trips to CERN, NASA and European Universities are organized within the scope of overseas education consultancy for students in Sınav Anatolian and Science High Schools. With the student exchange programs in these dormitories, some of the students who wish can continue their education like in countries such as the USA and Canada.

LESSON - HOMEWORK - STUDY - EXAM

Course distributions in Sınav High Schools are arranged in such a way that students are fully prepared for the exams in addition to their school courses and they do not need any other support. Lessons in Sınav High Schools are taught from "Theme-Explanation Modules" and "Question Bank Modules" specially prepared by Exam Publications. For this, more lessons, more studies and more exams are done with full-time practice. In Sınav High Schools, as in Sınav Journal Classrooms, practice exams are frequently applied in order to increase test practice, measure success and identify missing achievements through detailed analysis. All students who attend courses and exams; On the other hand, determined students or classes must participate in additional work, study, homework completion and question solution. Studies and additional studies are determined by the school according to the needs of the student or class.

Benefits Of Practice Sınav in Exam Academy

  • It allows students to practice the real exam before taking the real exam. It allows students to increase their test practice. It measures the knowledge level of the student. It strengthens the student's level of interpretation of exam questions. It provides support for the student to overcome exam stress and reduce exam anxiety. It helps them to use and manage time well. It makes them familiar with ÖSYM-style questions prepared by the Student Selection and Placement Center. Increases competition with new generation questions. It helps a student who regularly participates in practice exams to see the success graph better. It helps to measure the success of the student and to analyze the missing aspects of the student for the subjects that are missing in the exam results. It has positive effects on mentors and guidance teachers in identifying the missing subjects and following up the students. The development of the student is supported by analyzing the success graph.

How Do Practice Exams in the Sınav Academy Benefit the Student?

Rehearsing for the exam is very necessary in order to eliminate the unknown aspects of a real exam environment that will be encountered for the first time, not to make mistakes in the exam, and to manage time and stress effectively in the real exam. The student who takes the practice exam before the real exam knows what to do in the real exam. This is why a special and great importance is given to Practice Exams in Sınav Colleges.
